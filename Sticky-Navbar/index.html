Welcome to the ultimate pasta-making experience! This page will guide you through a simple and delicious recipe for creamy garlic pasta. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, you'll love how easy and tasty this dish is!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic(minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley(optional,for garnish)
  • Grated parmesan cheese(optional)

Steps

  1. 🍝Boil the Pasta:

    Cook pasta in salted water until al dente.Drain and set aside.

  2. 🧄Saute Garlic:

    In a pan, heat olive oil and saute minced garlic until golden.

  3. 🥛Add Cream:

    Pour in creame,stir, and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.

  4. 🥘Combine:

    Add pasta to the pan.Toss gently to coat with sauce.

  5. 🧂🍽️Season & Serve:

    Add salt, pepper, and garnish with parsley or chesse.Serve Hot!

Serving Suggestions

  1. 🥖Serve with Garlic Bread:

    Pair your pasta with warm, crispy garlic bread on the side. The buttery crunch adds a perfect texture contrast to the creamy sauce.

  2. 🥗Add a Fresh Side Salad:

    A light green salad with vinaigrette dressing balances the richness of the pasta. Try tossing together lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and olives for a refreshing touch.

  3. 🍷Beverage Pairings:

    A glass of chilled lemonade or iced tea is perfect for daytime meals. If you're having a fancy dinner, a mild red wine complements the creamy garlic beautifully.

  4. 🧀 5. Garnish for Extra Flavor:

    Sprinkle some freshly grated parmesan cheese and chopped parsley on top just before serving. A dash of chili flakes adds a spicy kick for those who like it hot.

Tips

  • Use freshly minced garlic for better flavor.
  • Don’t overcook the pasta — al dente is key!
  • Add a splash of pasta water to make the sauce silkier.
  • Want it spicy? Add chili flakes while sautéing garlic.
  • Use a non-stick or heavy pan to avoid burning the sauce.
